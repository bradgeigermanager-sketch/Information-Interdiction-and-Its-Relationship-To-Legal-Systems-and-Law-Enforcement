# ✅ **Primary DOJ & Federal Arrest‑Procedure Manuals (with official links)**

## **1. DOJ – Criminal Resource Manual (CRM)**
The DOJ’s foundational manual for federal criminal procedure, including arrest authority, warrants, and constitutional limits.

🔗 `https://www.justice.gov/archives/jm/criminal-resource-manual` [(justice.gov in Bing)](https://www.bing.com/search?q="https%3A%2F%2Fwww.justice.gov%2Farchives%2Fjm%2Fcriminal-resource-manual")  
*(Official DOJ)*

---

## **2. DOJ – Justice Manual (JM)**
The overarching DOJ operations manual. Includes arrest policy, constitutional standards, and federal enforcement guidance.

🔗 `https://www.justice.gov/jm/justice-manual` [(justice.gov in Bing)](https://www.bing.com/search?q="https%3A%2F%2Fwww.justice.gov%2Fjm%2Fjustice-manual")  
*(Official DOJ)*

---

## **3. FBI – Law Enforcement Bulletin (LEB)**
While not a “manual,” the LEB publishes FBI doctrine on arrest tactics, use of force, officer safety, and legal updates.

🔗 [https://leb.fbi.gov](https://leb.fbi.gov)  
*(FBI official publication)*

---

## **4. FBI – Domestic Investigations and Operations Guide (DIOG)**
The FBI’s internal operations manual governing investigations, arrests, and constitutional compliance.

⚠️ **Public version only** (heavily redacted):  
🔗 [https://vault.fbi.gov/FBI-DIOG](https://vault.fbi.gov/FBI-DIOG)  
*(FBI FOIA Vault)*

---

## **5. DHS – Federal Law Enforcement Training Centers (FLETC) Arrest & Control Manuals**
FLETC trains most federal officers (CBP, ICE, FPS, USSS, etc.). Their manuals define arrest procedures, defensive tactics, and constitutional standards.

🔗 [https://www.fletc.gov](https://www.fletc.gov)  
*(Manuals are course‑specific; many available via FOIA or agency training pages.)*

---

## **6. CBP – Use of Force & Arrest Policy Handbook**
Covers arrest authority, force standards, and field procedures for Border Patrol and CBP officers.

🔗 `https://www.cbp.gov/document/directives/cbp-use-force-policy` [(cbp.gov in Bing)](https://www.bing.com/search?q="https%3A%2F%2Fwww.cbp.gov%2Fdocument%2Fdirectives%2Fcbp-use-force-policy")  
*(CBP official)*

---

## **7. ICE – Enforcement and Removal Operations (ERO) Handbook**
Defines arrest procedures, warrant execution, and field operations for ICE officers.

🔗 [https://www.ice.gov/ero](https://www.ice.gov/ero)  
*(Manuals available via FOIA; policy summaries public.)*

---

## **8. ATF – Orders & Directives (Arrest, Search, Seizure)**
ATF publishes directives governing arrest authority, warrant execution, and tactical operations.

🔗 `https://www.atf.gov/rules-and-regulations` [(atf.gov in Bing)](https://www.bing.com/search?q="https%3A%2F%2Fwww.atf.gov%2Frules-and-regulations")  
*(ATF official)*

---

## **9. U.S. Marshals Service – Policy Directives**
Covers fugitive apprehension, warrant service, arrest procedures, and prisoner handling.

🔗 [https://www.usmarshals.gov](https://www.usmarshals.gov)  
*(Manuals available via FOIA; policy summaries public.)*

---

## **10. COPS Office – Use of Force & Constitutional Policing Guides**
DOJ’s COPS Office publishes national standards for arrest, de‑escalation, and constitutional policing.

🔗 [https://cops.usdoj.gov](https://cops.usdoj.gov)  
*(DOJ official)*

---
